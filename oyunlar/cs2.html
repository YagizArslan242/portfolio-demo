🎮 Pro Tactics Room

Counter-Strafing: While moving left or right, quickly press the opposite direction key and release movement. Your character stops instantly and your shots become perfectly accurate.

💡 Hidden Tips & Tricks

Due to the new sub-tick system, make sure to use a jump-throw bind when throwing grenades. This ensures your utility lands in the exact same spot every time.
