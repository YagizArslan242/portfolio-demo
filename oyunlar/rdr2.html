Red Dead Redemption 2

🎮 Pro Tactics Room

Best Free Horse Location: As soon as Chapter 2 begins, head to Lake Isabella in the snowy northwest region of the map. There you can find and tame the white Arabian horse, one of the fastest horses in the game.

💡 Hidden Tips & Tricks

When engaging enemies in Dead Eye mode, your weapon magazine is automatically refilled. This allows you to skip reload animations and gain a tactical advantage in combat.
