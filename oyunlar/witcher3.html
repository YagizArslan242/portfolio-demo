The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

🎮 Pro Tactics Room

Quen Shield: Always activate the Quen sign before combat. It absorbs the first incoming hit completely, making it extremely useful on higher difficulties.

Gourmet Skill: Unlock the “Gourmet” ability in the skill tree. It makes food regenerate health for 20 minutes from a single item.

💡 Hidden Tips & Tricks

Before taking monster contracts, always check the Bestiary. Applying the correct oils to your sword increases your damage output by up to 25%.
